5th Annual
'Music on the Mountain'

This annual event started on July 24th of 2015. Art Lane, Sheffield Music Boosters president and chairman of Music on the Mountain was inspired by an event in Johnsonburg called, ‘A Blast in the Burg’. This event hosted several open class DCI corps that performed there.

The Sheffield music boosters committee thought it would be a great idea to bring this type of event back to the Sheffield area. The Sheffield community have a history of marching band corps performing associated with the fire department’s summer festival and parade.

Although organizing the event was difficult and strenuous, the result inspired the committee to not let it be a one-and-done event. Preparations were made for the next year, and the rest is history.

Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

  Can I video or audio record the perfomances?

The short answer is NO. According to DCI guidelines, "...due to copyright restrictions based on federal law, video and audio recordings even for personal use are not permitted without permission from DCI or the corps that is being recorded."

  Can I take pictures at the event?

The Short Answer: Yes. According to DCI guidelines, "...family, friends and fans may take still photos of DCI events for personal use only. The images may not be sold or otherwise distributed on a Web site or in print, without the written permission of Drum Corps International or the corps that was photographed."

  How does the free shuttle work?

Patrons who drive can park at Saint Anthony's church (Route 6, west of sheffield school) and catch a bus shuttle from there. Volunteers will direct you to a bus when it is available.

  When / How can I get my tickets

Tickets purchased online will be available at main ticket booth the day of the event. Tickets will not be printed or mailed.

  When / How can I pre-order event T-Shirts

You can purchase event t-shirts online on ticket sales page. They can be picked up at main ticket booth the day of the event. Shirts will not be mailed.
