3rd Annual "Music on the Mountain"

The 3rd Annual occurence of this event will be the largest show in the region. More corps than ever before are going to be performing on August 4th. 5 of which are within the top   10 DCI Drum Corps in the US Open Class


  • 7th Regiment - New London, CT
  • Blue Devils B - Concord, CA
  • Legends - Kalamazoo, MI
  • Louisiana Stars - Lafayette, LA
  • Music City - Nashville, TN
  • Raiders - Burlington, NJ
  • Southwind - Mobile, AL

Gates will open at 4:30 and the show will start at 7:00

Tickets will be available for sale on June 1st 

Concession Stand opens at 4:30

Free shuttle is available from St. Paul's Center to Sheffield High, Starting at 4:30

Handicap Parking will be available on the hill

!No video or audio recording is permitted during the event!
