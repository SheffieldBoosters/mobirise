3rd Annual "Music on the Mountain"

The 3rd Annual incarnation of this event will likely be the largest show in the region. More corps than ever before are going to be performing on August 4th.

  • 7th Regiment - New London, CT
  • Blue Devils B - Concord, CA
  • Legends - Kalamazoo, MI
  • Louisiana Stars - Lafayette, LA
  • Music City - Nashville, TN
  • Raiders - Burlington, NJ
  • Southwind - Mobile, AL

Gates will open about 4:30 and the show will start at 7:00

Tickets will be available for sale on June 1st 

Concessions Stand opens at 6:00

Free bussing is available from St. Paul's Center to Sheffield High, Starting at 4:30

Handicap Parking will be available on the hill
